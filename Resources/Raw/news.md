# IndoorCO2Map News

## Welcome

Welcome to IndoorCO2Map! This is the first public release of the completely overhauled App.
This page will contain important news regarding the App and Project, and sometimes also general things concerning indoor air quality.

### What's New or improved

**More Reliable Location Search**
The App now uses our own system to find the locations in your vicinity. This System is - unlike the previous solution - extremely reliable and also a lot faster (usually needs way less than a second). Your exact GPS coordinate is never submitted as your phone itself calculates the map tile your are positioned in (roughly 2x2 km in size) and then requests that and if needed neighbouring tile. Which tile you request is also not even logged the way the system is set up.
The small disadvantage of this new approach is that we only update the data daily for cost reasons, therefore there is an option in the settings

**Transit Mode Improvements**
You can as previously record CO₂ measurements during public transit journeys. Select your route and stations, and the app will guide you through the measurement. A live map preview shows the selected route so you always know exactly what you're measuring.

**Recovery Mode Improvements**
If the app is closed or crashes during a recording, it will automatically attempt to reconnect to your sensor and resume where it left off when you reopen it. You can also trigger recovery manually or abort and resume later — your unfinished recording is kept safe until you decide.

**Longer Measurements**
Measurement length is no longer artificially limited — you can record for as long as your sensor's internal history allows (although we hope you don)

**Sort, Search & Distance**
Building results can be sorted by distance or alphabetically, and there is a search option to quickly find a location.

**Better Sensor Selection and Support**
You now can pick any Sensor nearby in the user interface. Improved Support for the Inkbird-IAM T1 and Airspot Health. Aranet4 now also works with very old Firmware. Airvalent is fully supported the same as before.s

**History & Export**
The history page shows all your own recordings. They can be exported (as an SQLite database) and imported again, making it easy to transfer your dataset to another device. History is stored locally on your phone only and can be disabled in Settings. All your recordings can also be displayed on a Map.

**Delete Faulty Measurements**
Made a mistake or recorded something you don't want to submit? Individual measurements can now be deleted directly from the History page before or instead of submitting them.

**Settings Menu for customisation**
There is a Settings Menu which you can use to adjust a lot of different things to your liking.

**Statistics Page**
This is still in a very early stage and just showing the total amount of measurements by country so far. Will be expanded on in future

### Visual Overhaul

The app received a significant visual refresh. Please let me know how you like it!

### Final Thoughts for now

The IndoorCO2Map dataset keeps growing — thank you to everyone contributing measurements!
There is a lot more in the works in the upcoming months: Inclusion of live data from stationary sensors, website improvements and a few other smaller things.
Stay tuned, and thanks for working together towards improving indoor air quality worldwide!
